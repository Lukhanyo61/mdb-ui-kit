Backabuddy Fundraising campaign

Our initiative, The ZAM DriveForward Initiative 2024, is dedicated to providing driving education to those who face barriers due to financial constraints and other challenges. We believe that empowering these young individuals with the skills and knowledge to obtain their driver's license will be a head start in opening doors to countless opportunities, significantly shaping their futures, their communities and family environments. Our goal as the ZAM Foundation is to assist at least 15 beneficiaries in 2024.

Learners' License

If you hold a learner’s licence you are allowed to drive only when you are supervised by a licensed driver. If the category of vehicle you are driving requires a professional driving permit, the licensed driver must also hold a professional driving permit. A learner’s licence is a permit that proves that you have basic knowledge of a motor vehicle and the rules of the road. The learner’s licence is valid for 24 months and cannot be extended.

Driver License Testing Centres

The City has 18 driving licence testing centres (DLTCs) located at central points throughout Cape Town. Every month we process an average of 5 000 learner and 5 300 driving licence tests. The centres also issue and renew professional driving permits and regular driving card licences for more than one million road users. Find out more about driver's licence test waiting times in Cape Town.

Our Program Structure

Our design approach is very organized to ensure satisfaction for our esteemed clients.

1

Open Application

•An application process is opened to allow all those who wish to enroll to submit their applications.
• Forms are distributed via various medium including FB, email, whatsapp and word of mouth.
• Forms are also sent to prospective organisations that work with young people who fit the criteria.

2

Select Qualifying Beneficiaries

•The purpose of the interview is to ensure that the correct beneficiaries are enrolled based on the information on their enrolment forms.
• The interview also serves to ensure that beneficiaries meet all the requirements

3

Interview Selected Beneficiaries

• The purpose of the interview is to ensure that the correct beneficiaries are enrolled based on the information on their enrolment forms.
• The interview also serves to ensure that beneficiaries meet all the requirements

4

Induction of selected beneficiaries

• Beneficiaries who have been selected for the learners’ license program will be interviewed in order to familiarize themselves with what is expected of them by the foundation.
• The interview also provides a platform for the foundation to know the beneficiaries better.
• Beneficiaries will be briefed on exactly what they are to expect from the foundation.

5

Learners’ lessons phase.

At this phase the beneficiaries are introduced to the respective driving school(s) at which they will attend their lessons for the learners’ license.
• Upon completion of the lessons and confirmation of readiness beneficiaries will make appointment for examination at their nearest DTLC.
• Completion and regular attendance of these lessons is compulsory.
• Beneficiaries are expected to complete their allocated learners’ lesson within a period of 4 weeks.

6

Post learners license debrief and skills workshop.

•Those beneficiaries who have been selected for the driver’s license phase are introduced to the respective driving schools for their driving lessons in the code of their choice.
• Upon completion of driving lessons and confirmation of readiness from instructors the beneficiaries make examination appointments at their nearest DTLC.

7

Driver’s license lessons phase.

• The purpose of the interview is to ensure that the correct beneficiaries are enrolled based on the information on their enrolment forms.
• The interview also serves to ensure that beneficiaries meet all the requirements

8

Post driver’s license debrief and continuous engagement

The phase includes continuous engagement with beneficiaries after their completion of the program as they navigate the work of job searching or employment.
