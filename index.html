Our Vision

To assist in the development of young people from disadvantaged backgrounds by providing access to necessary skills, development and promote education.

Our Mission

To provide a vehicle through which young people from disadvantaged backgrounds can be assisted to acquire skills that are necessary for employment seeking.

What we do

We provide access to learner's and driver's license training for youths from disadvantaged communities in order to enhance their chances of employment.

Our latest star!

The latest champion to join an illustrious group of young people who have, thru the ZAM Foundation program, attained their driver’s license is Luthando Valtyn. Luthando is the epitome of the saying, “dynamite comes in small packages”. Amongst the group of 10 beneficiaries in his cohort Luthando was one of 2 who did not succeed in the learner’s license exam the 1st time. True to his never say die attitude Luthando went back and passed his learner’s licence the 2nd time around. Not only that, as if to prove a point, Luthando went on to be among the first 2 beneficiaries to pass their driver’s license amongst his cohort. The ZAM Foundation congratulates Luthando for being a true champion and carrying the spirit of #payingitforward
